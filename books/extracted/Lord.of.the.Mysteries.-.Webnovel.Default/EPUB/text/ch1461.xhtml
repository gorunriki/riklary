Side Story: Rebellious Teenager

Note: It was published as an exclusive side story of Lord of Mysteries in the “Taiwanese Physical Book, Lord of Mysteries 01: Commission of Gold Pound” that covers from Web Novel Chapter 213: Another Look to Chapter 242: Bakerland.

This is a Bullet Point Paraphrasing Summary by Windvally, not the complete translation of the story.

Intis Southern Region, The Town of Nosil.

beautiful day is about to beginning.

Danitz said as he smiled at his friends around him. He is currently sitting atop metal fences situated near the edge of the town.

beautiful day!

His friends shouted loudly, appearing very energetic.

For teens like them, their clothing isn’t exactly the best in quality, but most definitely trendy, their choices closely resembles the current tending styles of Trier.

Of course, they were only following, but not matching, because there’s a very big difficulty that’s preventing them from doing so - No money!

  • Parents stated he’s 16 now and needs to take up responsibilities. They talked with the servant head working for the owner of a grape farm in the town. Danitz could be an apprentice servant.

  • Danitz wants to go to Trier and be rich

  • The next day Danitz slipped out of home before his parents could catch him.

  • He met up with his friends told him a new group of sailors and a bard arrived at the town’s local bar.

  • They went to the bar together.

The town’s bar almost have no interior decorations, only the wall was painted, and a few pictures cut from old magazines and newspapers were plastered on the wall.

At the bar counter, above the alcohol shelf, hanged the head of a giant snake, already white and cleaned to the bone.

That empty eye socket, is large enough to swallow the mouth of a normal adult, causing every drunkard who visits this bar to lower their head unconsciously when meeting its eyes.

Danitz was no exception.

According to what he knew, this giant snake skull was brought back from the Southern Continent by this bar’s owner. It’s that man’s souvenir for remembering many years of adventure.

That’s also why, even though this mister is rather quiet and introverted, no one dares to have greedy thoughts about his bar.

Even if a mafia from the big cities come to this place, when they see that giant snake skull, that owner’s ridiculous and exaggeratedly muscular arms, legs, and just muscle in general, most would probably wisely choose to interact peacefully. After all, a small town’s bar isn’t exactly that profitable, it’s not worth the trouble.

Danitz was always a little disappointed by this.

It’s not that he wanted a fellow native to be bullied, but rather he desired to witness how strong the bar owner is, as someone who used to work as a mercenary and adventurer on the Southern Continent.

In a way, the owner could be counted as a legendary person of the small town of Nosil. No competition within the past 30 years at the very least.

He left this place at a very young age, headed towards the Southern Continent for adventure. Around his middle age, he returned to his home town, carrying with him a decent fortune, a body of muscle, and a giant snake skull. He opened up a bar, married a widow, and had a kid.

He stayed absolutely silent in regards to his nearly 20+ years of adventurous life on the Southern Continent. No matter who asked, he only responded with unimportant things to change the subject.

Danitz was very disappointed by this.

However, Danitz once heard from a friend who live close to the bar owner, that the man will always do one of two things every single night: He either drink until it’s nearly morning before dropping, or fuck his wife until very very late into the night.

He seemed to be afraid of entering sleep.

If I have a beautiful wife, I will also screw her up and down until late into the night…Dantiz mumbled to himself in a low voice, while keeping an eye out for the sailors his friend mentioned.

that much money was able to surprise all of you? There are many more legends on the seas.

a certain location on the Berserk Sea, the treasure of The God of Death is waiting. It is waiting for a person with a very specific, special key to open it. And that person? He will become the new God of Death….On the seas, every single individual is chasing after the [Fountain of Youth, just one single sip from it, will grant a person eternal youth.Not only will he never growing old ever again, but he will also become extremely powerful…]

raised their head and looked at the sky, that fire like cloud looked like a seas of crimson, wide and glittering.

said he doesn’t want to come out and see you, because he will get angry every time he sees you.

Danitz’s mother said while wiping away some tears on the corner of her eyes.

Danitz cursed that stubborn old man with a few sentence inside his heart, he hugged his mother, and turned around to walk towards the waiting caravans.

As the caravans started moving, after traveling about 10-20 meters, Dantiz turned his head around unconsciously, his old man was standing next to his mother, not knowing when he already came out.

A bright smile appeared on Danitz’s face, he waved his right hand at them, and shouted silently inside his heart:

, Mother, I will return after becoming rich!
