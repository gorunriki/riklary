Foreword

Circle of Inevitability (宿命之环) is the second book in the Lord of the Mysteries series, written by Cuttlefish That Loves Diving. It began serialization on Qidian in March 2023. Set six years after the end of the first book, it explores the world of Intis and the approaching Apocalypse from a new perspective.
